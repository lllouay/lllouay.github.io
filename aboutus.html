About Us

At our Italian restuarant,we strive to create an authentic and immersive dining experience experience that transports you to the heart of Italy. With a deep-rooted passion for Italian cuisine, we are dedicated to showcasing the rich flavors,traditional recipes,and warm hospitality that define the essence of Italian dining

Our Popular Chef

Carlo Cracco

Cracco, who was born in Vicenza, Italy, has been cooking innovative Italian dishes in Milan since 1986. His dishes are not the customary Italian meals that you might see from Gino D’Acampo but rather incorporate fresh, inventive ideas that give them more flair than the normal dish.

Massimo Bottura

Bottura, who was born in Modena Italy,His food has garnered praise from many sources over the years and he has continued to bring Italian culture out through his food. Unlike Giorgio Locatelli , Bottura infuses his dishes in a modern and groundbreaking way that brings the old Italy to the new world.

Gino D’Acampo

With over 10 locations in the Lebanon serving his dishes, D’Acampo has spread his love of food in a major way. He brings the tastes of his native Napoli in the south of Italy to his food and keeps them simple and traditional.

De Laurentiis

De Laurentiis was born in Rome, Italy, and it doesn’t get any more Italian than that. She grew up spending most of her time in the kitchen, learning how to cook as well as spending time in her grandfather’s restaurant DDL Foodshow where she observed many of the traits of Italian cooking.
